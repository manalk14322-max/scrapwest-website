About Scrapwest

A UAE-standard scrap trading company focused on speed, transparency and reliable customer service.

Professional scrap buying across UAE

Scrapwest helps customers sell valuable scrap materials without confusion, delays or unclear pricing. The company is positioned for the UAE market, where customers expect fast communication, reliable pickup and a professional process from the first WhatsApp message to final collection.

We serve customers in Dubai, Sharjah, Abu Dhabi and the wider Emirates. Our work covers AC scrap, metal scrap, car scrap, electronics scrap, cable scrap, copper, aluminium, brass, steel and mixed industrial materials. Whether the job is a small pickup from a villa or a larger commercial clearance from a warehouse, the goal is simple: make scrap selling easier, faster and more profitable.

How we build trust

Trust is created through clear communication. Scrapwest encourages customers to send images, location and material details through WhatsApp so the team can respond quickly. A site survey may be arranged when required, especially for bulk scrap, machinery, AC units or construction material. Once the material is reviewed, the customer receives a practical offer based on type, quantity and current market value.

  • UAE phone and WhatsApp contact for immediate response.
  • Serving All Emirates for stronger business credibility.
  • Real scrap images used throughout the website.
  • Fast pickup messaging for urgent requirements.
  • Clean service pages built for Google UAE visibility.

This website is designed to reflect a modern UAE corporate industrial company: clean, minimal and high trust. The focus is not decoration. The focus is conversion, SEO, speed and clarity for customers who are ready to call or WhatsApp.
